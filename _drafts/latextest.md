Without the &= :

$$
\begin{align*}
    A = B\\
    this = that
\end{align*}
$$

With the &= :

$$
\begin{align*}
    A &= B\\
    this &= that
\end{align*}
$$

Without the &= :

$$
\begin{align*}
    C = D\\
    one = two
\end{align*}
$$

With the &= :

$$
\begin{align*}
    C &= D\\
    one &= two
\end{align*}
$$
